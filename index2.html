About Us

Our Community

Burnside High School Basketball is dedicated to creating an inclusive and competitive environment for all student-athletes. Our program is designed to nurture talent, foster teamwork, and build character both on and off the court. We believe that basketball is not just a sport but a platform for personal growth and development.

Our coaching staff consists of experienced professionals committed to providing top-notch training and mentorship. We focus on skill development, physical fitness, and tactical understanding, ensuring that every player reaches their full potential. Through rigorous training sessions and competitive gameplay, we instill discipline, perseverance, and a love for the game.

Whether you are a beginner or an advanced player, Burnside Basketball offers a supportive community where you can grow your skills, form lasting friendships, and represent your school with pride. Join us in embracing the spirit of competition and teamwork!

Winning is not EVERYTHING

Regardless of whether the goal is to win, learn new skills, make friends or just have fun, there is definitely something for everyone. Our students compete with distinction locally and nationally. We encourage and support both participation- orientated athletes and elite athletes.
